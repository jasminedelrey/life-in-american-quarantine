Our Digital Habits

Among the people that the author surveyed and interviewed, below you can find more information about their digital lives.


Ranking of digital activities

  1. Social media
  2. Media (podcasts/music/YouTube)
  3. Movies/TV Shows
  4. News
  5. Online Shopping

When asked about their digital tendencies, many students expressed that they were sure to be conscious of their news intake. One student stated that they purposefully joined Discord and Snapchat in order to connect with people during the remote school year. Many others used the Internet to escape from reality by streaming and binge-watching movies and TV Shows.
